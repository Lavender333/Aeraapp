- `alice@example.com` / `AeraDemo!2026`
- `david@example.com` / `AeraDemo!2026`

## 1) Role Purpose

General Members maintain accurate household readiness and submit reliable safety/needs updates during incidents.

## 2) What Members Should Be Able to Do

- Complete onboarding and preparedness profile
- Keep household and emergency contact data current
- Submit help/safety status through guided flow
- Track personal readiness checklist

## 3) Hands-On Workflow

### Step A: Login and Verify Profile

1. Sign in
2. Open Settings/Profile fields
3. Confirm address, phone, emergency contact

**Pass condition:** profile fields are complete and realistic.

### Step B: Preparedness Setup

1. Review household members
2. Update medical/accessibility details
3. Confirm consent checkbox

**Pass condition:** trainer confirms no critical field is blank.

### Step C: Incident Help Flow

1. Open Help Wizard
2. Complete safety and situation steps
3. Submit with clear needs

**Pass condition:** request is submitted with coherent details.

## 4) Member Errors to Prevent

- Leaving emergency contact incomplete
- Entering vague hazard descriptions
- Submitting without updating changed household conditions

## 5) Member Quick Quiz

- Where do you update transportation access and mobility status?
- What should you do if your household conditions changed this week?
- When should you submit a help request?
